I'm a developer who lives in the terminal. Most of my day happens inside `tmux` sessions, split between neovim, a shell, and whatever's compiling. I run Arch (btw) because I like knowing what's on my machine and why it's there.

I care about systems that are simple, fast, and correct -- in that order. I'd rather read a man page than a Medium article. I think the best interfaces are the ones that get out of your way.

This site is a place to put thoughts that outgrow commit messages.
